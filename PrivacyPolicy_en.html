Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 2 August 2025

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Helvi (“App”, “we”, “us”) collects, uses, and protects your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

  • Email address and profile data for account creation (if you register).
  • Usage data and app interactions via Firebase Analytics.
  • Crash logs and device info via Firebase Crashlytics.
  • Event viewing, saving, and browsing data (anonymous if not logged in).
  • Images loaded and cached using SDWebImageSwiftUI.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide, personalize, and improve the App and services.
  • To communicate with you about your account or support requests.
  • To diagnose and fix app issues.

3. Third-Party Services

  • Firebase Auth: user account management (Google LLC).
  • Firebase Analytics: usage evaluation, performance improvement.
  • Firebase Crashlytics: error and crash reporting.
  • SDWebImageSwiftUI: image display, no personal data collection.

4. Data Sharing and Security

  • No selling/sharing of personal data for advertising.
  • Google (Firebase) acts as a data processor on our behalf.
  • Industry-standard safeguards applied.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

  • Data retained while you use the App or as required by law.
  • You may delete your account; anonymized data may remain.

6. Children’s Privacy

  • Helvi’s content is rated 4+ on the App Store.
  • Accounts are intended for adults (parents/guardians). Children may use the App under adult supervision without creating an account.
  • We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child provided personal data, contact us and we will delete it.
  • Where an account is created: the account holder must be 16+ in the EU/CH (or 13+ elsewhere), or a parent/guardian acting on the child’s behalf.

7. Your Rights

  • Rights to access, correct, delete, port, or object to data processing.
  • Withdraw consent anytime by deleting your account.

8. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy. Significant changes will be announced in the App.
