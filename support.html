Quick Help

  • Can’t log in? Check your email spelling, try resetting your password, and ensure you have network access.
  • App crash or freeze? Update Helvi to the latest version and restart your device. If it persists, email us with steps to reproduce.

Account & Data

  • Delete account: In the app, go to Settings → Account → Delete Account. If you can’t access the app, email alice.turcanu91@gmail.com from your registered email.
  • Data requests (GDPR/FADP): You may request access, correction, deletion, or portability of your data by contacting us.
  • Privacy: See our Privacy Policy.
